Abstract

In this paper we use a hybrid macroeconometric model to forecast brazilian data. The model is
based on a real business cycles model with a ﬁrst order VAR process for the measurement errors.
The estimation is performed via maximum likelihood with the aid of the Kalman ﬁlter. Despite the
simplicity of the DSGE model used, the forecasts of the hybrid model are signiﬁcantly more precise
than the forecasts generated by a ﬁrst and second order VAR. Thus, showing that the DSGE model
can signiﬁcantly contribute to VAR forecasts.
