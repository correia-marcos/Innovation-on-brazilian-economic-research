Abstract 
The objective of this paper is to analyze the credibility of the inflation targeting regime in Brazil

expectations to central target set up by monetary authority. To this end, a revision was made on 
some previously proposed indices of credibility for the country, summing up to five measure-

the credibility and the deviations of inflation expectations from the central inflation target. In

variables related to credibility, we found correlation coefficients greater for the new index. It
