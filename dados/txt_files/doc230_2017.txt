lar Pharmacy Program (PFPB) for the drug treatment of chronic noncommunicable diseases (DCNT),

demonstrate that the expansion of the Program occurred according to socioeconomic and demographic
factors that are strongly related to market and unfolded from the principle of equity that guides as
actions of the national health policy.
